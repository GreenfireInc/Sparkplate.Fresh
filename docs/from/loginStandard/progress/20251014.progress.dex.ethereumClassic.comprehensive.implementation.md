## üîç Unique Ethereum Classic Characteristics

### **Ecosystem Properties**
- **Proof of Work:** Uses Ethash PoW (unlike Ethereum's PoS)
- **Code is Law:** Maintains immutability philosophy
- **EVM Compatible:** Full compatibility with Ethereum tooling
- **Lower Fees:** Generally cheaper than Ethereum
- **Smaller DeFi:** Limited ecosystem compared to Ethereum

### **Integration Considerations**
1. **Limited Subgraphs:** Few indexed data sources
2. **Lower Liquidity:** Native DEXs have smaller TVL
3. **Instant Exchanges:** Often better for larger swaps
4. **Security History:** Past 51% attacks require longer confirmations
5. **Contract Verification:** Always verify addresses from official sources

### **Best Use Cases**
- **Native Swaps:** ETCswap (V2 & V3 features)
- **Largest TVL:** HebeSwap (~$350k)
- **Cross-Chain:** Instant exchanges (SideShift, ChangeNOW, SimpleSwap, StealthEX)
- **Price Feeds:** ETCswap TWAP oracles, HebeSwap oracle
- **NFT Trading:** HebeSwap NFT marketplace
